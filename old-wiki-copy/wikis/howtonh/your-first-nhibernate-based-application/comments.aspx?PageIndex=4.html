By: nofb Posted on 03-21-2009 10:20

As a general rule, it is best to use

 copy "$(ProjectDir)..\..\SharedLibs\sqlce*.dll" "$(ProjectDir)$(OutDir)"

than

 copy $(ProjectDir)..\..\SharedLibs\sqlce*.dll $(ProjectDir)$(OutDir)

to take the possible space characters into account, especially with Windows.

It is most probably the problem linker85 encountered.
